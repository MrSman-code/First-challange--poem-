Mr Romantic

In sickness, appetite fades from her sight,
Craving peppery fish through the darkest night.
Clouds gather, ominous and vast,
I journey West, the die is cast.
Long waits and travels, to the farthest shore,
To purchase that dish, she longs for even more.
Then, in the East, she must make her retreat,
Deliver that fish, with romantic sight,
As rain falls fast, and the cold winds greet.
Homeward-bound, wet, and shivering cold,
In the downpour's embrace, her story unfolds.
